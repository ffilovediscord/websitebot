About Sentinel

Sentinel is designed from the ground up to be your server's ultimate guardian. We understand the challenges of maintaining a safe and welcoming environment on Discord, especially as your community grows. Our bot leverages advanced algorithms and real-time threat detection to ensure that your members can interact without fear of disruption.

From preventing join-raids to filtering out unwanted content, Sentinel works tirelessly in the background, allowing your moderators to focus on community engagement rather than constant threat management. Invite Sentinel today and experience peace of mind.

Bot Commands

Sentinel uses slash commands for easy interaction. Here are some common ones:

  • /warn [user] [reason] - Issues a warning to a user.
  • /timeout [user] [duration] [reason] - Temporarily mutes a user.
  • /ban [user] [reason] - Permanently bans a user from the server.
  • /kick [user] [reason] - Removes a user from the server.
  • /fulllockdown - Fully locks down the server, preventing all new messages and joiners.
  • /slowmode [channel] [duration] - Sets a slowmode in a specified channel.
  • /bulkdelete [amount] - Deletes a specified number of messages from the current channel.
  • /help - Displays all available commands and their usage.
